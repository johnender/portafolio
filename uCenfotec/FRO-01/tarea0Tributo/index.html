HomeWork 0: Tribute

Ronnie Radke

December 15, 1983 - present

Ronald Joseph Radke is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, musician, and record producer.

    The following is a time line of Radke life

  1. 2004: Formed Escape The Fate with Bryan Money, Max Green and Robert Ortiz.
  2. 2006: They signed in early 2006 with Epitaph Records after winning a radio contest judged by My Chemical Romance, which launched their career.
  3. 2008: With the help of Nason Schoeffler,Radke formed the band known as Falling in Reverse.
  4. 2010: Upon his release from prison Radke announced his return to recording
  5. 2010: Falling In Reverse released their first single "Raised by Wolves".
  6. 2011: Falling In Reverse released their first album "The Drug in Me Is You".
  7. 2013: Falling In Reverse released their second album "Fashionably Late".
  8. 2015: Falling In Reverse released their third album "Just Like You".
  9. 2017: Falling In Reverse released their fourth album "Coming Home".
  10. 2018: Falling In Reverse released the songs called "Losing My Mind" and "Losing my life".
  11. 2019: Falling In Reverse released the songs called "Drugs" and "Popular Monster".
  12. 2020: Falling In Reverse released the songs called "The Drug In Me Is Reimagined" and "Carry On".
  13. 2021: Falling In Reverse released the songs "I'm Not a Vampire (Revamped)".
  14. 2021: Falling In Reverse announced two shows called "Live From The Unknown" that would be streaming at the end of April 2021.
